About

Myself

I am a freelance film maker/ Videographer based in Aberdeen, Scotland. I am a recent Media graduate from the Robert Gordons Univeristy, and focus primarily on video production services. I encompass a wide range of projects from short films and personal pieces through to documentaries, corporate promos and events across Scotland. This website charts my journey with Videography and how it can be used to help grow you as a client.
